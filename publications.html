In Preparation

Who Benefits? A Scoping Review and Taxonomy of Human–Plant Interactions in Urban Environments

WEN Minjie
A scoping review developing a five-category taxonomy of beneficiary structures in human–plant interaction (HPI) artworks: Anthropocentric, Phytocentric, Relational, Ecocentric, and Indeterminate benefit. The paper analyzes more than fifty works across academic literature, specialist media-art archives, and curated artwork documentation.
Status: Manuscript in preparation — Frontiers Research Topic "Re-Imagining Mediated Human–Building Interaction and Sensory Environments: Volume II."
